About

Who We Are

KAIST Global is an independent nonprofit public interest corporation established in New York. While not an official subsidiary of KAIST, key figures from KAIST who share the institution's global vision participate at the advisory and board level, maintaining continuity and alignment with the broader KAIST community.

Working in partnership with KAIST Alumni, we are building a platform that sustainably connects the KAIST community on a global scale ‚Äî bridging alumni, academia, and industry across borders.
